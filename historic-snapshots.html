Did you know that Brightspot automatically stores all the content you publish? Saving, referencing and re-purposing work you've already done makes for a more efficient publishing process. Historical snapshots save you time by giving you an easy way to name and find the things you want to save.

To save a version of something in the past, click on it in History, found in the right rail. You will get a prompt that says, "This is a past revision..." Click on the link for "Edit Revision Name" and name your historical snapshot. It's that easy. Once saved, your re-named version will appear at the top of the History widget in the right rail.

Summary: Historic Snapshots


Brightspot makes finding and re-using past versions of your content simple. There is no limit to how many snapshots are saved. The ability to refer to past content means that you save time when re-creating content. That's efficiency.
