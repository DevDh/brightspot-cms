Start by logging into Brightspot. Once logged in, open a new browser window and navigate to any page on the front-end of your site. You will notice In the top right-hand corner you will find two options, shown in blue.

      • Edit Inline – Clicking this open the inline editor on the page you are viewing
      • Edit In CMS – Clicking here opens the CMS on the content edit screen for the page you are viewing

Edit All Content
Every editable piece of content is highlighted with a pencil icon.
Access the CMS
Get access to the content within the CMS in one click.
Familiar Interface
Use the same interface as the full CMS.
Save Instantly
Make your edits and click save. Refresh the page to see the updates instantly.
