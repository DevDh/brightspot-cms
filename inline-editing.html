Brightspot 2.0 provides a new and improved inline editor tool. Formerly known as the ‘bookmarklet’ this tool allows editors to access and edit their content from the front end of their site, with a fully functional interface for editing text, images or SEO.

Start by logging into your instance of Brightspot. Once logged in, open a new browser window or tab and navigate to any page on the front-end of your site. You will notice in the top right-hand corner you will find two options, shown in blue.

• Edit Inline – Clicking this open the inline editor on the page you are viewing
• Edit In CMS – Clicking here opens the CMS on the content edit screen for the page you are viewing

Edit All Content
Every editable piece of content is highlighted with a pencil icon.
Access the CMS
Get access to the content within the CMS in one click.
Familiar Interface
Use the same interface as the full CMS.
Save Instantly
Make your edits and click save. Refresh the page to see the updates instantly.


Summary: Inline Editing


The goal of the inline editor is to provide you with a quick and easy way to make changes to your content as you see the need on your site. Everything that can be edited on a page within the CMS is also available to be edited inline, using the same intuitive user interface and tools.
